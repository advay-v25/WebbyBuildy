# WebbyBuildy Product Context

## What is WebbyBuildy?
A premium mechanical keyboard and UI/UX design showcase platform built with Next.js, showcasing high-end keyboards with immersive 3D/video interactions.

## Target Audience
- Design-conscious tech enthusiasts (25-45 years old)
- Mechanical keyboard aficionados
- UI/UX professionals looking for inspiration
- Premium tech product buyers

## Product Lane
**Premium Brand/Showcase** — Not a SaaS tool, but aspirational brand marketing + portfolio

## Voice & Tone
- **Premium, sophisticated, attention to detail**
- Show don't tell (let visuals speak)
- Emphasis on craftsmanship and build quality
- Smooth, refined interactions (no harsh animations)
- Technical accuracy (specs, materials matter)

## Key Features/Sections
1. **Hero Showcase** — Hero keyboard with video/3D
2. **Process Section** — Manufacturing/design process (pinned animations)
3. **Mechanical Keyboard Gallery** — Product showcase
4. **Specifications & Details** — Material, switches, sound
5. **Call-to-Action** — Shop or learn more

## Success Metrics
- Page immersion and time spent
- Smooth animations that feel premium
- Mobile responsiveness (mobile gamers too)
- Accessibility doesn't compromise premium feel

## Brand Values
- **Craftsmanship** — Detail-oriented, not mass-produced
- **Precision** — Exact, high-quality interactions
- **Elegance** — Minimalist, refined aesthetic
- **Performance** — Smooth 60fps, no jank

## Anti-References (What NOT to be)
- Generic e-commerce (boring product grids)
- Startup template defaults (purple/blue, card soup)
- Overly playful/fun (wrong for premium segment)
- Slow or janky animations (kills premium feeling)
- Cluttered information hierarchy

## Accessibility Requirements
- WCAG AA minimum (premium doesn't mean exclusionary)
- Keyboard navigation throughout
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion
- High contrast text (4.5:1 minimum)
- Focus states visible for keyboard users
